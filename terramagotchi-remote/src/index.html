Remote Info

This is the remote app for interacting with a Terramagotchi display.

  • To interact with the environment, press any of the buttons displayed.
  • Each button with a + will add that particle to the display at a random location.
  • Added seeds will be a random type of plant.
  • The change time button will move time forward by about 6 hours equivalent.
  • We hope you enjoy the display!

To dismiss this message, either press the button below or the X in the corner.
